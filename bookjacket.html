Flipped Book Jacket

Print / Book Jacket design
Indesign, Illustrator

For my GrC 439: Book Design Technology class, I also had the opportunity to re-design a book jacket of choice. I decided to choose Flipped by Wendelin Van Draanen. I sketched the front cover illustration and vectorized it in Illustrator. I really wanted to capture the liveliness and spirit of the novel. Therefore, I chose imagery that reflected the story plot. I also used bright colors because it was a young adult novel that would hopefully attract the appropriate age demographic.
