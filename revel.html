Revel Magazine

Print/ Digital Magazine Layout & Design
InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop

REVEL Magazine is an eight page iteration of a lifestyle magazine directed towards Asian-Americans. The purpose of REVEL is to feature artists/actors in the Asian entertainment industry, while delivering fashion, food, and beauty related topics that pertain to the Asian-American demographic. REVEL was part of the Four-Forty Magazine that was created in my magazine design class. The intention of the project was to produce an actual magazine that was required to include a cover, masthead with advertisement, table of contents, department/editorial spread, feature spread, and a back page advertisement.

After creating a print version of REVEL Magazine, I converted the printed magazine into a user-friendly iPad-specific digital publication created with the Adobe Digital Publishing Suite. Adobe Digital Publishing Suite allows you to publish dynamic, interactive content in the form of an app. The publication features vertical and horizontal layouts, external links, scrollable text frames, pan and zoom images, and multi-state objects.
