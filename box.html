Cal Poly Tailgate in a Box

Print / Packaging design
Illustrator

For my IT 408 class, our class was given the task to design a tailgating package made of corrugated fiberboard for the AICC 2015 Student Packaging Design Competition. All of our graphics were digitally printed on the Xante Excelagraphix 4200 and the Konica Minolta c1070. Our graphics reinforce the spirit and branding of Cal Poly. Since we decided to choose soccer as our tailgating sport, all of our graphics were aimed at conveying the love of the sport and Cal Poly’s pride in soccer. The outside of the box is kept simple and geometric, with a call to action for alumni to come and tailgate with Cal Poly. Inside, the box lid converts to a foosball table and is modeled to look like a soccer field stadium with spectators cheering on Cal Poly versus UC Santa Barbara (Cal Poy’s biggest soccer rival).
