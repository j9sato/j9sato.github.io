Oasis Menus

Print / Menu Design
Indesign, Illustrator

During my marketing internship at University Graphic Systems, I was given the opportunity to design and produce menus for the restaurant Oasis in downtown San Luis Obispo. I decided to design the menus around the look and feel of the restaurant, which was primarily a deep red. I also worked with them on branding and I helped to simplify their logo. In the end, I created their lunch/dinner menu and their window menu.
